App Description:

_____________________________________________________

This application automates the practice process, allowing unlimited practice sessions. All sessions are recorded as audio files for self-review or professional evaluation. It offers various modes: a simulated exam mode (showing only face-to-face and part 2 questions), a full practice mode (displaying all part 1 to part 3 questions with note-taking), and individual part practice modes (part 1 or part 2+3).


"Images of the simulated computer-based speaking test"


Basically, the application uses speech recognition technology to identify the speaker. Speech recognition technology only recognize the human voice so đon't worry about the surrounding sound .If they pause for a period of time, the system will recognize that the user has stopped speaking and will proceed to read the next question. In practice, during a speaking test, the examiner will only stop when the candidate stops speaking, and if the candidate stutters for a long time, the examiner will continue to ask questions. If users encounter situations where the examiner interrupts them during practice, they should consider improving their fluency, pronunciation, and speaking speed to match the requirements. Improving during practice will also enhance speaking abilities in real-life situations or during the speaking test.


The application has two main modes: simulated speaking mock test and practice. In the simulated speaking mock test mode, users will experience a test that simulates up to 90% of the real exam, helping them to better prepare by becoming familiar with the test format and managing their anxiety. In practice mode, all questions are displayed, allowing users to feel more at ease. This is particularly helpful for those with limited listening skills, as knowing the questions in advance can be beneficial; however, note that in the actual test, no questions will be displayed except for the questions in part 2.


The recordings will also be saved after completing the test. Each recording will be named according to the test mode and the start time, making it easier for users to access their data.
