INTRODUCTION:

_____________________________________________________

In the virtual speaking room - VSR, users can experience the real exam of the IELTS speaking computer-based test and strengthen their mentality and essential speaking skills to prepare for the real test.

Image1: Practice test interface


Solution:

_____________________________________________________

It's a solution that saves time, money, effort, and manpower for the speaking practice phase, which normally requires an additional person to act as an examiner. Moreover, the app offers various modes such as full test practice or individual practice for part 1 or part 2+3. Therefore, the app is suitable for both beginners and experienced learners. The freedom to practice without limits helps learners improve their speaking skills significantly.

More

Advantage:

With an easy-to-use, user-friendly interface and nearly 90% of the processes automated and similar to the actual speaking test, it helps users become familiar with and practice so they won’t feel unfamiliar during the official exam.


Notice:
Please note that the software does not have a grading function because AI CANNOT replace humans in grading speaking tests. For optimal learning and progress, it is recommended to use the recorded clips as materials for self-review or to seek feedback from more experienced individuals !!!

App Description:

_____________________________________________________

This application automates the practice process, allowing unlimited practice sessions. All sessions are recorded as audio files for self-review or professional evaluation. It offers various modes: a simulated exam mode (showing only face-to-face and part 2 questions), a full practice mode (displaying all part 1 to part 3 questions with note-taking), and individual part practice modes (part 1 or part 2+3).
